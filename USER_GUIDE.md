## üí∞ Budget Search

**Find the perfect destination that fits your budget!**

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Navigate to Budget Search**
   - Click "Budget Search" in the header
   - Or click "Start Planning" on the homepage

2. **Fill in Your Details**
   ```
   Total Budget: Enter amount in PKR (e.g., 50000)
   Trip Duration: Number of days (e.g., 5)
   Starting City: Where you're traveling from (e.g., Lahore)
   Travel Type: Solo, Couple, Family, or Group
   ```

3. **Click "Find Destinations"**
   - Wait 2-3 seconds for results
   - System searches database for matching destinations

4. **View Your Results**
   You'll see **Top 3 Destinations** with:
   - **Destination name** and region
   - **Rating** and weather conditions
   - **Cost breakdown**:
     - Transport costs
     - Food expenses
     - Local transport
     - Activities budget
   - **Seasonal warnings** (if applicable)
   - **Best months** to visit

5. **Explore Options**
   Each destination card shows:
   - **Score** - How well it matches your criteria
   - **Package types** - Budget/Moderate/Luxury
   - **Seasonal event badges** - Festivals or special events

6. **Take Action**
   - Click **"Generate Plan"** to create detailed itinerary
   - Click **"Get Price Alerts"** to track price drops
   - Click **"Explore Nearby"** to see nearby destinations

### Currency Conversion
- Results show prices in PKR by default
- Click the currency dropdown (top right of results)
- Select from 23+ currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.)
- Prices update instantly

## üó∫Ô∏è Trip Planner

**Get AI-powered day-by-day itineraries!**

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Open Trip Planner**
   - Click "Plan Trip" in header
   - Or click "Generate Plan" from budget search results

2. **Enter Trip Details**
   ```
   Destination: City or place name
   Days: Trip duration (1-30 days)
   Travel Style: Adventure, Relaxation, Cultural, Budget, or Luxury
   Budget: Daily budget preference
   Preferences: Optional (e.g., "love hiking", "vegetarian food")
   ```

3. **Use Map Selector (Optional)**
   - Click "üìç map selector" link
   - Map opens showing South Asia region
   - **Search location**: Type in search box
   - **Click on map**: Select precise coordinates
   - Click "Select" on location marker

4. **Submit Form**
   - Click "Generate My Trip Plan"
   - AI generates personalized itinerary (15-30 seconds)
   - Loading animation shows progress

5. **Review Your Trip Plan**

   **Overview Card:**
   - Destination name
   - Duration
   - Travel style
   - Budget range

   **Weather Card:**
   - Current temperature
   - Weather conditions
   - Humidity and wind speed

   **Cost Breakdown:**
   - Accommodation
   - Transportation
   - Food & Dining
   - Activities
   - **Total Estimate**

   **Daily Itinerary:**
   - Day-by-day activities
   - Morning/Afternoon/Evening sections
   - Activity descriptions
   - Local tips and recommendations

6. **Save Your Trip**
   - Click **"Save to Database"** button
   - Trip saved for future reference
   - Can edit later from profile

7. **Export as PDF**
   - Click download icon
   - PDF generated with full itinerary
   - Automatically downloads to your device

8. **Explore Nearby Places**
   - Scroll to bottom
   - View "Nearby Popular Places" card
   - See 5 recommended destinations within 300km
   - Click "View" to plan trips to nearby places

## üîî Price Alerts

**Get notified when trip prices drop!**

### Setting Up Alerts:

1. **From Budget Search Results**
   - Click "Get Price Alerts" on any destination card
   - Confirmation message appears

2. **What Gets Tracked**
   - Current total trip cost
   - Daily price checks (midnight UTC)
   - 5% drop threshold

3. **View Your Alerts**
   - Click bell icon in header
   - Dropdown shows:
     - Price drop alerts (if any)
     - Seasonal event alerts
     - Alert count badge

4. **Alert Types**

   **Price Drops:**
   - Shows destination name
   - Old vs new price
   - Savings amount
   - "View Details" button

   **Seasonal Events:**
   - Festival/event name
   - Best months to visit
   - Alert message
   - Priority (High/Medium/Low)

5. **Manage Alerts**
   - Currently view-only
   - Future: Unsubscribe option in dropdown

### Seasonal Events

Automatic alerts for:
- **Dolmen Mall Lahore** - Winter festivals (Dec-Jan)
- **Basant Festival** - Lahore (Feb-Mar)
- **Cherry Blossoms** - Hunza (Mar-Apr)
- **Snowfall Season** - Murree, Naran (Dec-Feb)

## üí° Tips & Tricks

### Budget Search Tips:

‚úÖ **Be Realistic with Budget**
- Include all costs: transport, food, activities, accommodation
- Add 10-15% buffer for unexpected expenses

‚úÖ **Try Different Travel Types**
- Solo = Lower costs
- Family = Higher accommodation but split transport
- Group = Best value per person

‚úÖ **Check Seasonal Warnings**
- Red boxes show months to avoid
- Green boxes show best months
- Plan accordingly for weather

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Setting budget too low (no results)
- Very long trips with small budgets
- Peak season travel on tight budgets

### Trip Planner Tips:

‚úÖ **Use Preferences Field**
- "Love hiking and nature"
- "Vegetarian food only"
- "Interested in historical sites"
- AI customizes recommendations

‚úÖ **Save Multiple Versions**
- Save different styles (Adventure vs Relaxation)
- Compare costs and activities
- Choose best fit

‚úÖ **Export to PDF**
- Print for offline access
- Share with travel companions
- Keep as backup

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Too many days (>14) without breaking into segments
- Vague preferences
- Skipping the weather check

### Alert System Tips:

‚úÖ **Set Alerts Early**
- Prices can drop 10-20% seasonally
- Track multiple destinations
- Compare before booking

‚úÖ **Check Seasonal Events**
- Plan around festivals
- Avoid monsoon/extreme weather
- Get best experience

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Setting alerts too close to travel date
- Ignoring seasonal warnings
- Missing event opportunities

### Search History Tips:

‚úÖ **Review Before Booking**
- Compare all destinations you searched
- See evolution of your preferences
- Find forgotten options

‚úÖ **Use Re-Run Feature**
- Update prices monthly
- Check if destinations still fit budget
- Track trends

### Testimonial Tips:

‚úÖ **Write Detailed Reviews**
- Minimum 20 characters, but aim for 100+
- Include specific experiences
- Mention costs, activities, tips

‚úÖ **Add Helpful Info**
- Best time you visited
- What made it special
- Would you return?

‚úÖ **Upload Quality**
- Honest reviews help community
- Balanced (pros and cons)
- Specific recommendations

‚ùå **Avoid:**
- Generic comments ("Nice place")
- All negative or all positive
- Spam or inappropriate content

## üö® Troubleshooting

### Common Issues:

**Issue: "No results found" in Budget Search**
- **Solution:** Increase budget or reduce days
- Try different starting city
- Check if destination exists in database

**Issue: Map not loading**
- **Solution:** Refresh page
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser

**Issue: "Login Required" messages**
- **Solution:** Create account or login
- Some features need authentication
- Continue as anonymous for basic features

**Issue: Testimonial stuck on "Pending"**
- **Solution:** Wait 24-48 hours for approval
- Check content meets guidelines
- Contact support if >48 hours

**Issue: PDF download not working**
- **Solution:** Allow popups in browser
- Check download folder
- Try different browser

**Issue: Alerts not showing**
- **Solution:** Click bell icon in header
- Check if logged in
- Subscribe to price alerts first
